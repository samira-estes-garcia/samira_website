Hi, I'm Samira Estes-Garcia!

I’m a full-stack web developer out of San Antonio, TX.

I graduated from the University of Texas with a B.S in Nutritional Sciences, I learned a lot but ultimately decided it wasn’t for me. In February 2019, I enrolled in a Front End web development course with Thinkful and graduated in Aug 2019. Soon after, I received a full scholarship with Out In Tech X Flatiron for Flatiron's Full Stack Software Engineering Immersive program. I am now freelancing while actively searching for web dev roles and going through Flatiron's course!

Coding started out as a passion project, a new skill to pick up while I was thinking about what to do next. What I thought was going to be fun hobby turned out to be the launch of a fun career! I love building mobile and web apps and just find it so satisfying to see my creations come to life in real time. The problem solver in me loves debugging and the creative in me utilizes a design and responsive first approach. I am always looking for new opportunities and challenges.
